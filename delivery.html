Problem Statement

E-commerce businesses often face late deliveries due to logistics inefficiencies, leading to customer dissatisfaction, increased returns, and higher operational costs. Predicting potential delays before shipment can significantly improve order fulfillment.

Aim

To design a machine learning system that predicts the risk of delayed deliveries using historical order and logistics data, enabling proactive intervention and improved supply chain planning.

Approach

  • Collected and preprocessed historical order and delivery data.
  • Engineered features such as delivery distance, shipping method, and order size.
  • Built classification models to predict late vs. on-time deliveries.
  • Evaluated models with accuracy, F1-score, precision, and recall metrics.
  • Deployed insights to support logistics and operations teams.
